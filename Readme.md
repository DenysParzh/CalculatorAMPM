# Research of the two-dimensional state space of the matrix shift register

## Requirements
- Python 3.11
- Django 5

## Interface components
- The values rank of the matrices F, A and B - L, n and m
- Coefficients of polynomials fF(x), fA(x) and fB(x)
- Structural matrices F, A and B and their theoretical and experimental periods  T<sub>F</sub> , T<sub>A</sub> and T<sub>B</sub>
- Matrices inverted to matrices F, A and B => F<sup>-1</sup>, A<sup>-1</sup> and B<sup>-1</sup>
- The rank of the matrix of the initial states of the MSR and initial states as the Smith normal form
- Matrix element number (i, j)
- Generator states
- Spectrum of Hamming weights
- Graphs of cyclical ACF
- Step-by-step iterations
